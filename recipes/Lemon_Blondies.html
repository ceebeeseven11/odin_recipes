Lemon Blondie Recipe

Ingredients

Blondie

  • 3/4 C salted butter, softened
  • 1 C granulated sugar
  • 1/4 C light brown sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 large lemons, juiced and zested
  • 1 1/4 C all-purpose flour, spooned and leveled
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda

Glaze

  • 2 C powdered sugar
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice, equal to about 1 medium lemon
  • 2 Tbsp heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 deg. Fahrenheit. Line a 9x13 inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In large bowl, beat salted butter, granulated sugar, and light brown sugar until well creamed.
  3. Mix in eggs, one at a time, until completelt combined.
  4. Add lemon juice and zest, beat until well mixed.
  5. Add flour and baking powder, mix on low speed and beat just until fully combined.
  6. Pour into baking dish and bake for 25-30 mins.
  7. Take out blondies when done and set aside to cool to room temp.
  8. While cooling, make glaze by whisking the powdered sugar, lemon juice, and heavy cream together until smooth.
  9. Pour glaze over cooled blondies and let glaze set.
  10. Cut and serve. Enjoy!
