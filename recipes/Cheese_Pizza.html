Cheese Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 prepped pizza dough
  • 1/3 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Position an oven rack in the lower third of oven and preheat to 475 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, Stretch the dough into a 12 inch circle. Transfer to a non-stick baking sheet.
  3. Spread sauce onto dough. Leaving a 1 inch border for crust. Then sprinkle the cheese on.
  4. Bake for 12-14 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
  5. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes, then cut and serve.
